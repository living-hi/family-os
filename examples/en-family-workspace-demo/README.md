# Family OS Workspace

This is a private household workspace. Keep personal household information here, not in the Family OS skill package.

## Main Flow

1. Put raw material in `00-inbox/`.
2. Keep current priorities in `01-dashboard/`.
3. Store long-running domains in `02-areas/`.
4. Track time-bound efforts in `03-projects/`.
5. Record durable choices in `04-decisions/`.
6. Preserve stable facts and preferences in `05-memory/`.
7. Review periodically in `90-reviews/`.
8. Archive old material in `99-archive/`.
