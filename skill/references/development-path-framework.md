# Household Development Path Framework

Use this framework to design a family's path without hard-coding any private facts into the skill.

## 1. Stabilize

Goal: reduce fragility.

Focus areas:

- cashflow and emergency reserve
- health and caregiving basics
- household division of labor
- core documents and insurance
- near-term risk list

## 2. Systematize

Goal: make household operations repeatable.

Focus areas:

- family workspace and inbox routine
- project tracking
- recurring bills and renewals
- weekly and monthly reviews
- decision records

## 3. Grow

Goal: expand capability and quality of life.

Focus areas:

- income and career capacity
- parenting and education capacity
- investment discipline
- health routines
- home environment and support network

## 4. Build Optionality

Goal: increase choices.

Focus areas:

- location flexibility
- career flexibility
- education options
- time autonomy
- resilient assets and skills

## 5. Stewardship

Goal: preserve and transmit what matters.

Focus areas:

- family values
- long-term care planning
- intergenerational knowledge
- asset and document continuity
- annual reflection and renewal

When adapting this framework, read the user's local workspace and produce a concrete path with dates, constraints, and next actions in the workspace, not in the skill.
